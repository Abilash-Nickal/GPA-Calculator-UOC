Data Import Guide

  1. Open your Result Sheet in the student portal.
  2. Select & Copy the entire results table.
  3. Navigate to INPUT RESULTS in this app.
  4. Paste the copied text and click PROCESS DATA.

1. Copy your results from the University Portal

2. Paste into the Input Results section

Data Storage & Cloud Sync

Never Lose Your Progress!
- Guest Mode (Local Save): By default, clicking Save Progress securely stores your data right inside your web browser.
- Cloud Sync: Enter your Student ID and click LOGIN / SYNC. Once logged in, your tracker data is securely synced to the cloud.

Install as a Mobile App (PWA)

🍎 For iPhone (Safari)
1. Open Safari and go to your tracker URL.
2. Tap the Share button (square with arrow up).
3. Scroll and tap "Add to Home Screen".
4. Tap "Add" in the top right.
🤖 For Android (Chrome)
1. Open Chrome and go to your tracker URL.
2. Tap the Menu (three dots ⋮).
3. Tap "Add to Home screen" or "Install App".
4. Tap "Add" to confirm.

Core Features Overview

  • Cloud Sync & Local Backup: Save data locally or sync across devices by logging in.
  • Dashboard Overview: Real-time CGPA and SGPA trend analysis.
  • Master Data: Edit, reorder, and manually adjust course results.
  • Semester Overview: Level-by-level performance breakdown.
  • Target Tracker: Plan your path to success.
  • Data Import: Intelligent parsing from university portal results.
